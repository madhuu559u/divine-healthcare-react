Medicaid Coverage

Virginia Medicaid Covers Home Care at No Cost to You

If you or your loved one has Virginia Medicaid (including Medicaid Expansion), you may qualify for in-home personal care, respite, and companionship services at no out-of-pocket cost.

  • Ages 21+ with Medicaid coverage
  • Need help with daily living activities
  • Doctor's order or referral
  • We handle all DMAS paperwork
